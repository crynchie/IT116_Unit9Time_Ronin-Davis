This page changes its appearance based on the current time of day:

  • Morning (5am to 12 noon): Shows a morning image, a "Good Morning!" message, and a gold background color.
  • Afternoon (12 noon to 6pm): Shows an afternoon image, a "Good Afternoon!" message, and a dark orange background color.
  • Evening (6pm to 11pm): Shows an evening image, a "Good Evening!" message, and an orange red background color.
  • Night (11pm to 5am): Shows a night image, a "Good Night!" message, and a dark slate gray background color.

The copyright year updates dynamically based on the current year.
